Strategic Intelligence

Executive Takeaways

01
Performance
MPT matched the S&P 500 on total return with active Gen Z exposure

The Gen Z Digital Pulse portfolio returned 94.60% versus the S&P 500's 93.11% — a 1.49 percentage point outperformance using a genuine forward-looking 60-day sliding window. Every allocation decision used only data available at that moment — no hindsight, no future peeking.

02
Risk
Higher volatility and deeper drawdowns are the cost of concentration

Daily volatility of 1.70% versus SPY's 0.95% and a maximum drawdown of −36.77% versus −18.76% confirm the risk of a concentrated thematic strategy. The S&P 500 wins on Sharpe ratio (1.14 vs 0.74). For risk-averse clients, passive indexing remains superior. For aggressive growth mandates, our model delivers comparable returns with active brand exposure.

03
Culture
Both strategies independently confirmed the same top holdings

The MPT optimizer and the Moving Average Golden Cross strategy independently identified Spotify (81.2% Golden Cross), Amazon (75.6%), and Meta (74.8%) as their top signals. Two completely different mathematical approaches arrived at the same conclusion — a strong indicator of genuine Gen Z brand momentum, not just model artifact.

04
Brand Signal
Revenue growth separated real Gen Z brands from vibe picks

Stocks selected using Piper Sandler survey data and Google Trends acceleration — filtered by SEC revenue growth — consistently outperformed brand recognition alone. Celsius (102% YoY growth), e.l.f. Beauty (76%), and Dutch Bros (48%) were the clearest cases of cultural relevance directly translating into financial performance.

05
Strategy
The Moving Average strategy underperformed both benchmarks

The Golden Cross strategy returned only 59.45% — below both MPT (94.60%) and the S&P 500 (93.11%). Its Sharpe ratio of 0.60 was the lowest of all three. Holding an average of 13.1 stocks in equal weights diluted the impact of top performers. This confirms that disciplined optimization with fixed 10-stock selection beats rules-based signal trading for this universe.
